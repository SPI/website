SPI Reimbursement Request

After filling in this form please press the "Generate PDF" button, then email the resulting PDF to your projects SPI liaison.

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare an SPI Reimbursement Request Form

Use the form below to generate an SPI Reimbursement Request Form.

Ensure that the banking information is accurate as incorrect details are a major source of delays.

Save it in PDF format.

Step 2: Prepare an Expense Report

Use the XE Travel Expenses Calculator to prepare an expense report (even for non-travel expenses).

Enter Your Name, which must match the name in the SPI Reimbursement Request Form from Step 1.

Set Your Home Currency as appropriate.

Leave/set Credit Card @ 3%, Debit Card @ 5%, Foreign Cash @ 5%, Traveller's Cheque @ 2%.

Enter Receipt Details, one row per receipt, specifing the correct date, amount and currency of the transactions.

Save it in PDF format.

Step 3: Collect and order your Receipts

Collect your recipts in the SAME ORDER as the rows in the Expense Report from Step 2.

For paper receipts, scan them with a multi-function device or photograph them with your phone, converting to PDF.

Save them in PDF format.

Step 4: Prepare the Submission Package

Collect into a single PDF, the following:

  • from step 1, the SPI Reimbursement Request Form
  • from step 2, the Expense Report
  • from step 3, the Ordered Receipts

Ensure that the entire Submission Package can be easily understood as poor quality submissions are a major source of delays

Consider using the pdfunite utility from the poppler-utils package to merge the individual PDF files into a single PDF file

Usage: 

pdfunite step1.pdf step2.pdf step3a.pdf step3b.pdf ...  step3n.pdf ReimbursementRequest.pdf

Step 5: Email the Submission Package

Prepare an email having these attributes:

  • from: you
  • to: treasurer@rt.spi-inc.org
  • subject: Reimbursement Request for «short_description»
  • attachment: the ReimbursementRequest.pdf file prepared in step 4
  • body: (the text below) 
    By submitting this reimbursement request, I declare:
  - that I have prepared an SPI Reimbursement Request Form including bank account details,
  - that I have prepared an Expense Report using XE's Travel Expense Calculator,
  - that I have attached sufficient documentation substantiating my request,
  - that I seek reimbursement of expenses that are compliant with SPI and applicable Associate Project policies, and
  - that I have not sought nor will seek reimbursement of these expenses from any other source.
These expenses were incurred for the following reason:
  «long_description»

Replace «short_description» and «long_description» as appropriate.
